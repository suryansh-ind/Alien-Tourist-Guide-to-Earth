Frequently Asked Questions

Is Earth safe for my species?

Earth is classified as a Level 3 Risk Planet. While generally safe, precautions are necessary. The atmosphere contains microbes that may interact unpredictably with non-terrestrial biology.

What if humans discover my true form?

Immediately activate your camouflage device and retreat to a safe location. Contact Galactic Embassy through your subspace communicator. Do not reveal advanced technology.

Can I bring Earth specimens back home?

Collection of biological specimens is strictly regulated. Microorganisms and plants below Class 3 may be collected with proper permits. Animal specimens require special authorization.

How do I handle Earth's time zones?

Earth is divided into 24 time zones. Your universal translator can automatically adjust to local time. Remember to account for daylight saving time in some regions.
